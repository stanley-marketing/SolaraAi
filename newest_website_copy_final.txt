## section 6 — Solara directs you on camera.

**Headline:** *You've opened your camera and closed it a hundred times. Solara tells you exactly what to do.*

### The loss without it

You're not a videographer. You don't know where to stand, what angle, what light, what's "good enough." You shouldn't have to. You're running a business. So you've opened your camera, felt lost, closed it, and lost another week.

### How Solara solves it

When Solara asks you to film, it doesn't say *"film your pizza."* It says:

> *"Stand to the right of the oven, one meter back. Phone vertical at chest height. When I count you in, tilt down to show the pizza coming out. 5 seconds. Here's what it should look like."*

Then it shows you. A reference visual. A diagram of where to stand in *your* shop — because Solara learned your space during onboarding.

- Need someone to hold the phone? Solara tells you exactly what to ask for.
- Don't have anyone? Prop it on a shelf, a counter, a phone stand. Solara picks the spot for you.
- Need to speak on camera? The teleprompter inside the web app runs at your pace.

**Press record. Solara tells you if it worked. You're out.**

---

### And if speaking on camera is hard for you — we built the teleprompter for that exact reason.

Most business owners are not natural performers. Reading a 30-second monologue makes you stumble, sweat, restart. So we don't ask you to.

The teleprompter breaks every script into **one short sentence at a time**. You read one line. Stop. Read the next. Up to 5 takes per line. Solara picks the cleanest take and stitches them together so smoothly the viewer thinks you said it all in one breath.

You never memorize. You never perform. You just read short sentences — and Solara makes the result flawless.

### Why we built it this way

Directing is the entire reason humans had to physically be in your shop with an iPhone. The $2,000 a month was 90% the directing — telling you where to stand, when to start, when to do another take. We took that one job and moved it onto your screen — with a reference visual every time, a teleprompter that respects how nervous you are, and a workflow that takes the technical burden completely off you.

### The feeling

Filming stops being scary. You stop thinking. You just do the simple thing Solara asked for and walk away.
