## §5 — How the workflow actually works

The reason most SMB owners can't make content themselves isn't time. It's the hundred micro-decisions per shot: what angle, how far back, vertical or horizontal, what to say, how long, how to start, how to end.

That's the directing. That's what you were paying a manager for.

Solara does it through your phone.

**Step 01 — Solara sends the ask.**
One short message. What to film today. No long brief, no creative brainstorm.

**Step 02 — It shows you the shot.**
A shot brief opens: reference frame, framing rules, a teleprompter line if you're speaking. No guesswork. No deciding angles.

**Step 03 — You film what it asks.**
Short clip, straightforward action. The reference stays on screen the whole time. Film it, submit it, move on.

**Step 04 — Solara reviews the take.**
If it's clean, it's in the edit queue. If something's off — wrong thing in frame, someone walking behind you, the audio bad — it asks for one more. When the AI isn't sure, a human on our team steps in.

**You bring: short clips of your actual brand. Solara brings: everything else.**
